## About Us

**NCP Diaspora Alliance Germany** is a platform that brings together Bangladeshi diaspora members in Germany for constructive engagement, community coordination, advocacy, and nation-oriented initiatives.

Our goal is to organize diaspora voices around issues including:

- Bangladeshi policy matters  
- Social issues  
- Citizen rights  
- Diaspora rights  
- Community-driven collaboration and reform-oriented initiatives  

---

## Our Vision

To become an effective platform that helps organize the Bangladeshi diaspora around policy engagement, social issues, citizen rights, and especially diaspora rights.

---

## What We Do

- Connect Bangladeshi diaspora members in Germany
- Support civic and community initiatives
- Share updates, programs, and organizational activities
- Encourage structured collaboration and responsible participation
- Build a meaningful digital presence for our community
